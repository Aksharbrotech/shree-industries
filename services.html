Hard Anodising

Our Hard Anodising (Type III) process creates an extremely hard, abrasion-resistant coating on aluminum surfaces. This electrochemical process is essential for components exposed to harsh environments or heavy wear.

Key Benefits:

  • Extreme wear and abrasion resistance
  • Superior corrosion protection
  • Electrical insulation (dielectric properties)
  • Improved lubricant retention
