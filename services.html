Surface Treatment

Hard Anodising (Type III)

Our hard coat anodising process creates an extremely hard, abrasion-resistant coating on aluminum surfaces. Essential for components exposed to harsh environments, this electrochemical process enhances the natural oxide layer.

  • Hardness: Comparable to tool steel (60-70 Rockwell C).
  • Thickness: Controlled build-up from 25 to 100 microns.
  • Insulation: Excellent dielectric properties.
